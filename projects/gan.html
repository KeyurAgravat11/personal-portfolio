Food delivery App

  • Tech Stack: JAVA,XML,SQL
  • Github URL: Project Link

A food delivery app in Java is a software application that enables users to order food from various restaurants and have it delivered to their desired location. The app provides a user-friendly interface where customers can browse through a list of restaurants, view their menus, and select dishes they wish to order. Customers can customize their orders, specify delivery addresses, and make payments within the app. The food delivery app also includes features for restaurant management, allowing restaurant owners to manage their menus, update prices, and process orders received through the app. The app may incorporate additional functionalities such as real-time order tracking, customer reviews and ratings, promotions and discounts, and customer support

It may also include features for delivery personnel, allowing them to receive and manage delivery requests, track their routes, and communicate with customers. The food delivery app in Java integrates with external services like payment gateways and mapping services to facilitate secure payments and accurate delivery tracking. It provides a convenient and efficient solution for users to order food from their favorite restaurants and have it delivered to their doorstep
